LED Upgrade

The LED upgrade is one of the most popular upgrades for the 2024 Subaru Forester. This simple modification can make a big difference in your vehicle's overall appearance and functionality. By replacing the standard visor bulbs with LED lights, you can enhance the illumination of your Forester, creating a brighter, whiter, and crisper light.

LED lights are also more energy-efficient compared to traditional halogen bulbs. Using less power, they can extend the life of your vehicle's battery. Moreover, LED lights have a longer lifespan, so you won't need to replace them as often.

Door Projector Lights

One of the coolest and most unique accessories for the 2024 Subaru Forester is the door projector lights. These lights add a touch of style and flair to your vehicle by projecting a silhouette of the Forester onto the ground when the front doors are open. This looks incredibly cool and adds a level of sophistication to your Forester that is sure to turn heads.

Footwell Illumination Kit

This kit is designed to gently illuminate the dark corners of your Forester's front floor area with a soothing blue glow. The LED lights are integrated with the vehicle's light management system, ensuring they brighten and dim in sync with the interior lighting. This adds a touch of elegance to your Forester's interior and makes it easier to see and find items in the front floor area, especially in low-light conditions.

The Sport Grille allows you to create a custom look for the front of your vehicle. Additionally, the grille is built to last and can withstand harsh weather conditions, ensuring your vehicle is protected for a long time.

Auto-Dimming Mirror with Compass and HomeLink®

The upgraded auto-dimming mirror is designed to detect glare and darken automatically, ensuring a comfortable driving experience and protecting your vision. Moreover, it features an 8-point digital compass, providing accurate directional information at a glance.

One of the most notable features of the Auto-Dimming Mirror with Compass and HomeLink® is its HomeLink® functionality. This feature allows you to program three backlit buttons to operate various devices such as garage doors, security gates, home lighting, etc. Additionally, if programmed to a compatible opener featuring two-way communication, the mirror can keep you updated on the last status of your garage door, letting you know whether it's open or closed.

The cargo net can be used in two ways: you can anchor it to the sides of the cargo area to help prevent shifting from front to back, or you can lay it across the floor to help keep things from rolling around. Whether transporting groceries, sports equipment, or luggage, the Cargo Net ensures that your items stay in place and don't slide around while driving.

Cargo Separator

The Cargo Separator is a practical solution for keeping your cargo and passengers safe and secure.
