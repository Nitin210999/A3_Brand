Specs that Make the 2024 Acura TLX a Must Buy

Athletic Styling

The 2024 Acura TLX emerges as the quintessential choice for enthusiasts seeking the epitome of a best sports sedan. Melding performance and luxury seamlessly, the Acura TLX 2024 boasts an athletic styling that captivates onlookers both inside and out.

Within the cabin, the 2024 Acura TLX showcases refinement through the use of high-grade materials such as leather and genuine metal accents. The driver takes center stage in the wrap-around cockpit, embodying a design philosophy that prioritizes performance. The cabin exudes an aggressive, supercar-inspired character, further heightened by features like the Dynamic Mode dial, paddle shifters, sport pedals, and an available flat-bottom steering wheel.

The interior experience is elevated with a leather-trimmed interior featuring Premium Full-Grain Milano leather. The ergonomic sport seats, designed for both comfort and support, can be enhanced with Ultrasuede trim, providing a tactile performance aesthetic. Adding a touch of ambiance, the Iconic Drive Ambient Cabin Lighting offers 24 modes inspired by renowned racetracks worldwide, allowing drivers to tailor the atmosphere to suit any drive.

On the exterior, the 2024 Acura TLX showcases a sculpted design, characterized by a lengthy hood, broader body, and a lower, athletic stance that demands attention on the road. The available rear spoiler and exposed dual exhaust finishers accentuate the car's performative nature, amplifying its visual allure.

The Jewel Eye LED Headlights contribute to the exterior's appeal, maintaining a radiant, jewel-like appearance. Casting a wide, bright white beam that emulates natural sunlight, these headlights enhance down-road visibility while adding a stylish touch to the TLX's facade.

Completing the overall aesthetic, the Acura TLX 2024 offers a selection of premium paint finishes including Performance Red Pearl, Majestic Black Pearl, Platinum White Pearl, Apex Blue Pearl, and the 2024 Acura TLX Type S exclusive Tiger Eye Pearl. The application of these durable pigments in tinted clear coats results in a brilliant chromatic color with a deep, rich finish, adding the final strokes to the exterior styling of this exceptional sports sedan.

Driving Dynamics and Handling

The 2024 Acura TLX redefines the driving experience, setting the standard for what enthusiasts can expect from the best sports sedan in its class. The exceptional driving dynamics and handling of the Acura TLX 2024 are a testament to its commitment to performance and precision.

Engaging steering and responsive brakes form the foundation of the TLX's dynamic driving experience. Whether navigating tight corners or cruising down the open road, the 2024 Acura TLX ensures a connection between the driver and the road that is both thrilling and confidence-inspiring.

Under the hood, the 2024 Acura TLX offers two distinct engine options tailored to different trims. The 2-liter VTEC Turbocharged engine, generating 272 horsepower and 280 lb.-ft. of torque, powers the Technology and A-Spec trims with a front-wheel-drive (FWD) drivetrain. For the 2024 Acura TLX Type S, a commanding 3-liter Turbo V6 engine produces an impressive 355 horsepower and 354 lb.-ft. of torque, complemented by the superior handling of the Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) drivetrain. Both engines utilize a 10-speed automatic transmission.

The 2024 Acura TLX introduces versatility with its four driving modes: Comfort, Normal, Sport, and Individual. Exclusive to the Type S trim is the Sport+ mode, amplifying the driving experience to match the elevated performance of this variant.

The 2024 TLX's suspension system plays a crucial role in delivering precision handling and potent cornering grip on a variety of road surfaces. The double-wishbone front suspension, coupled with the available Adaptive Damper System, ensures a responsive and composed ride.

Bringing the stopping power to match its acceleration, the Acura TLX 2024 incorporates a highly sophisticated electro-servo brake system derived directly from the NSX supercar. This system ensures precision braking through corners, contributing to the overall dynamic prowess of the vehicle.

For those opting for the 2024 Acura TLX Type S, a host of performance-enhancing features awaits. Quad exhaust outlets boldly signify the potent power under the hood, while exclusive high-performance chassis bracing, a sport-tuned Adaptive Damper System, and a Brembo front 4-piston braking system work in harmony to deliver handling with pinpoint precision, making this awesome machine fun to drive.

Advanced Safety Features

AcuraWatch, a comprehensive suite of both available and standard advanced safety and driver-assistive technologies, equips your 2024 Acura TLX with a range of features designed to enhance driver awareness and mitigate the impact of potential collisions. Below are some of its notable features.

The Collision Mitigation Braking System (CMBS) serves as a vigilant ally, alerting drivers to potential frontal impacts and, when necessary, applying the brakes to either prevent or reduce the severity of a collision. This proactive system underscores Acura's commitment to proactive safety measures.

Road Departure Mitigation (RDM) further fortifies the safety net, notifying drivers of unintentional lane or roadway departures. In response, the system can apply steering torque and braking to aid in keeping the vehicle safely on its intended path, promoting a secure driving experience.

The inclusion of Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) with Low-Speed Follow is a testament to Acura's dedication to driver comfort. This system helps alleviate fatigue by maintaining a preset speed and distance from the vehicle detected ahead. The low-speed follow feature goes the extra mile, seamlessly bringing the vehicle to a complete stop if the detected vehicle ahead slows to a stop.

It's essential to note that, despite these advanced safety features, the driver retains the ultimate responsibility for safely operating the vehicle and avoiding collisions.

Innovative Technology

The 2024 Acura TLX is not just recognized as the best sports sedan for its exhilarating performance but also stands out as a technological marvel with an array of cutting-edge features. At the forefront of its tech offerings is the 12.3-inch Precision Cockpit Digital Instrument Cluster, providing drivers with a comprehensive and customizable display of essential information. This feature ensures that drivers have crucial data at their fingertips, enhancing both convenience and safety.

The infotainment experience is taken to new heights with the 12.3-inch Dual-Content Infotainment Display. This sizable touchscreen integrates seamlessly into the design, offering an intuitive interface for navigation, entertainment, and connectivity. With Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, the 2024 Acura TLX ensures a seamless integration of your smartphone, allowing you to stay connected and entertained while on the go.

Adding to the convenience is the wireless charging pad, providing a clutter-free solution to keep your devices powered up. The Wi-Fi hotspot ensures a connected experience for all passengers, allowing for seamless internet access on the road. Bluetooth connectivity and SiriusXM Satellite Radio further enhance the audio and entertainment options, catering to diverse preferences.

The True Touchpad Interface is a standout feature, offering an ergonomic touchpad that employs absolute positioning. This innovative technology allows drivers to control the 10.2-inch HD display with precision.

For those opting for the TLX A-Spec and Type S trims, the TLX becomes an audiophile's haven with the ELS Studio 3D Premium Audio System. This critically acclaimed system boasts 17 speakers, including 4 ultra-slim Highline overhead speakers, delivering a music studio-level sound quality that elevates the driving experience.

The 2024 Acura TLX Type S trim takes the tech experience even further with the exclusive 10.5-inch Head-Up Display (HUD). This advanced feature projects key information directly into the driver's line of sight, enhancing situational awareness and providing critical data without diverting attention from the road.

The 2024 Acura TLX emerges not just as a car but as a masterpiece of automotive engineering, solidifying its reputation as the best sports sedan in its class.
