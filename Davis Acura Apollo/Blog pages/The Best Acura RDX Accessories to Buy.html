The Best Acura RDX Accessories to Buy

Now that you have decided to buy an Acura RDX, you might also consider adding some accessories. Luckily, Acura offers a wide range of accessories to personalize your journey with your RDX. Whether you need extra storage space for adventurous road trips or want to protect your Acura RDX interior, Acura Genuine Accessories has got you covered. You can choose from options like roof boxes and trailer hitches for added convenience or opt for all-season floor mats, and rear seat covers to keep your RDX looking pristine.

One of the key benefits of buying Acura RDX accessories is that they are specifically designed for your car. This means they seamlessly integrate with its design and functionality, ensuring a perfect fit. Unlike generic accessories, Acura Genuine Accessories are precision-crafted to meet the high standards set by the brand. This not only enhances the aesthetics of your RDX but also provides added value and durability, making them a wise investment in the long run.

From practical accessories like cargo organizers and roof rack attachments to stylish upgrades like body side moldings and illuminated door sill trim, there is something for every RDX owner's taste and preference.

Wireless Charging Pad

Are you tired of dealing with tangled cords while on the go? The 2024 Acura RDX Wireless Charging Pad will solve this problem. You no longer have to worry about cords and adapters with its cord-free function. Additionally, the Wireless Charging Pad is designed ergonomically to be easily accessible so that you can charge your devices seamlessly while on the move.

Remote Engine Start System

This convenient feature allows you to start your car up to 328 feet away, ensuring your cabin is at the perfect temperature before you even step inside. With an integrated two-way remote featuring LED indicators, you can stay updated on your car's status without checking physically. Plus, the system integrates seamlessly with your car's security and immobilizer systems, ensuring you're always protected.

Heated Sport Steering Wheel

Keep your hands warm during winter commutes with the Acura RDX Sport Steering Wheel - Heated. With just a press of a button, this innovative feature warms up the steering wheel and adds a touch of comfort to your ride. The heating function is located conveniently on the left side of the steering wheel and can be easily activated when the ignition is turned on. The flat-bottom design gives a sporty look to your driving experience, while the contrasting red stitching provides a distinctive finish. Say goodbye to cold hands and hello to a more comfortable drive with the Acura RDX heated steering wheel.

Engine Block Heater

If you live or travel in areas that experience freezing temperatures, this essential accessory can make all the difference. Not only will it help your engine start faster in cold weather, but it will also greatly reduce wear and tear on your battery and engine. The solid brass-encased heating element is not only reliable but durable, ensuring you're protected for winters to come

Hands-Free Access Power Tailgate

Say goodbye to fumbling for keys or struggling with your hands full when trying to open or close your tailgate. With this innovative feature, you only need the smart entry remote and a simple kicking motion under the center of the rear bumper. It's like magic! Imagine effortlessly loading groceries, luggage, or any other cargo without ever having to put them down.

Logo Pattern Projector

This innovative accessory uses advanced LED lights to project the iconic Acura logo onto the ground upon opening your RDX's door, adding a unique and personalized touch to your car.

Illuminated Door Sill Trim Set

This nifty accessory serves a dual purpose - not only does it enhance the ambiance of your ride, but it also protects the lower door sill from unsightly scrape marks. Talk about form and function! But it gets even better. When you open your door, the trim's frame, graphics, and the iconic Acura logo light up, adding a cool factor to your entry and exit.

Crossbars

These crossbars are designed with convenience in mind, making them easy to install and remove from your RDX. They are essential for mounting Acura attachments that allow you to transport all your favorite gear, such as skis, surfboards, or any other equipment. You can trust their quality because they are made with high-quality aluminum and advanced plastics, offering a lightweight design without compromising strength and durability.

Door Edge Guards

Discovering those annoying nicks and scrapes on your car can be incredibly frustrating, not to mention expensive to fix. Fortunately, these door edge guards are designed to provide a sturdy and reliable barrier against these damages. They are color-matched to flawlessly blend in with your car's paint color, ensuring a seamless appearance.

Body Side Molding

Elevate the look and safeguard the finish of your Acura RDX with sleek and stylish body-side molding. Positioned strategically to offer maximum protection where your side panels extend out the farthest, this accessory is here to defend your car against door nicks and chips. No need to worry about parking lot mishaps or accidental bumps anymore.

20-in Black Diamond-Cut Alloy Wheels with Tires

Enhance the style and elegance of your RDX with these precision-cast wheels. The hub-centric design ensures a perfect fit for wheel balance, reducing vibrations and providing a smooth and comfortable ride. These wheels are an effortless upgrade that will instantly enhance the look and feel of your car.

Cargo Protector

Protect and preserve your Acura RDX's cargo area with the Acura RDX Cargo Protector. This essential accessory safeguards the original condition of your cargo area, including the sides and backs of the rear seats, while still allowing the seats to be adjusted independently.
