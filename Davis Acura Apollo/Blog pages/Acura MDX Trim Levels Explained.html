Acura MDX Trim Levels Explained

The 2023 Acura MDX is an incredible luxury midsize SUV that has captured the attention of automotive enthusiasts and families alike. Known for its striking design, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled performance, the MDX offers a range of trims to cater to various preferences and lifestyles. As you embark on your journey to find the perfect SUV, exploring the different trims of the 2023 MDX will reveal a world of possibilities tailored to your unique needs.

Each 2023 Acura MDX trim level boasts its own distinct set of features and enhancements, ensuring there is something for everyone. From the well-rounded base model to the sporty A-Spec, the MDX lineup offers diverse options that will impress even the most discerning buyer. Whether you prioritize performance, technology, or luxury, the 2023 MDX has a trim that will exceed your expectations and elevate your driving experience.

Below, we will discuss in detail the different trims of the 2023 Acura MDX, highlighting the key features that set them apart from each other.

MDX Base Trim

The 2023 Acura MDX offers an exceptional combination of luxury, performance, and advanced technology that sets the stage for an extraordinary driving experience. Under the hood, the MDX boasts a powerful 3.5-liter V6 engine that delivers an impressive 290 horsepower and 267 lb.-ft of torque, ensuring a responsive and exhilarating ride.

The interior is equally captivating, with a spacious cabin that features a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and infotainment displays, Acura Premium Audio System with 9 Speakers, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and AcuraWatch â€” a suite of advanced safety features. These remarkable features come standard, making the base trim an attractive option for those seeking luxury and innovation without compromise.

Beyond its impressive performance and technology, the base trim of the 2023 Acura MDX also provides an excellent level of comfort and convenience. With its Tri-Zone Automatic Climate Control, power-adjustable heated front seats, and premium materials throughout the cabin, the MDX ensures that every journey is a first-class experience.

Furthermore, the base trim's versatile seating and cargo configurations make it an ideal choice for families and adventure-seekers alike, offering ample space for passengers and gear.

Technology

The Technology trim builds upon the features of the base model with additional luxury and tech enhancements.

Inside, the cabin has sport seats with perforated Milano premium leather with contrast stitching, an interior color-matched headliner, and ambient LED lighting on the upper side of the door handles, the door handles itself, and the first-row foot well. Additionally, the Technology trim includes a GPS-linked climate control system that smartly adjusts the cabin temperature based on the changing environments and weather, further enhancing the overall comfort level inside the vehicle.

The 2023 Acura MDX Technology package also comes with an ELS Studio premium audio system with 12 speakers and Acura Navigation System with 3D View, which helps drivers reach their destination with ease.

For added convenience, the Technology trim also includes a blind spot information system, low-speed braking control, and front and rear parking sensors, providing drivers with increased awareness and confidence on the road.

A-Spec

The A-Spec trim adds sportier styling and exclusive design elements to the MDX, making it a popular choice for those seeking a more aggressive look.

A notable feature of the A-Spec trim is its bold and dynamic exterior styling, which includes a gloss black grille and LED fog lights. Its 20-inch Shark Gray wheels and dual exhaust outlets further accentuate the trim's athletic appeal, creating a distinctive and head-turning presence on the road.

Inside, the A-Spec trim continues to impress with its sport-inspired cabin, featuring a geometric patterned brushed aluminum trim, Sport A-Spec Seats with perforated Milano premium leather and Ultrasuede with contrast stitching and piping, heated and ventilated front seats, a leather-wrapped, flat-bottom sport steering wheel, stainless-steel pedals, and ebony headliner.

Beyond its captivating design, the 2023 Acura MDX A-Spec package includes an ELS Studio 3D premium audio system with 16 speakers and ambient LED lighting strips on the upper side of the door handle, the door handle, and the first-row foot well.

Advance

The Advance trim is a great choice for those who want a well-equipped and luxurious SUV. It offers a comfortable ride, plenty of power, and additional comfort and convenience features.

One of the most notable features of the Advance package is the sophisticated and inviting cabin, which boasts authentic open-pore wood trim, leather-wrapped heated steering wheel, and heated and ventilated front 16-way power seats with power lumbar support, thigh extension, and side bolsters. The Advance also has second and third-row charging ports and an ELS Studio 3D premium audio system with 16 speakers.

This particular trim has many ambient LED lighting strips, which can be found on the upper and lower sides of the door handle, the door handle, the first-row foot well, the instrument panel, the console, and the door pocket.

In addition to its focus on comfort and luxury, the 2023 Acura MDX Advance trim offers cutting-edge technology features. This includes Key Fob Activated Remote Engine Start with Vehicle Feedback and a head-up display that projects essential driving information onto the windshield.

The Advance package also includes roof rails and Power Lift Tailgate with Hands-Free Access and Walk Away Close, making it easy to access the cargo area even when your hands are full. Furthermore, the trim has a surround-view camera system that provides a 360-degree view of the vehicle's surroundings.

Type S

The 2023 Acura MDX Type S trim takes the luxury SUV experience to new heights with its performance-oriented features and striking design elements.

At the heart of the Type S lies the powerful 3.0-liter V6 turbocharged engine, delivering an impressive 355 horsepower and 354 lb.-ft of torque. This potent powertrain, combined with the Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system, ensures that the Type S provides an exhilarating and dynamic driving experience.

Drivers will also appreciate the Adaptive Air Suspension and Brembo front brake calipers, which offer precise handling and exceptional stopping power, further distinguishing the Type S from its siblings in the MDX lineup.

Inside, the cabin exudes a sense of refinement and sophistication with its Sport A-Spec Seats with perforated Milano premium leather and Ultrasuede with contrast stitching and piping, flat-bottom sport steering wheel, and Iconic Drive Ambient LED Cabin Lighting with 27 color themes.

Moreover, the Type S trim offers an array of awesome features, such as the 16-speaker ELS Studio 3D premium audio system, multi-view rear camera system, and Acura Navigation System with 3D View, providing drivers with an immersive experience.

Type S Advance

The 2023 Acura MDX Type S Advance trim represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance in the MDX lineup, offering a host of exclusive features that cater to the most discerning drivers. Building upon the already impressive Type S trim, the Type S Advance package adds several comfort and convenience enhancements that elevate the driving experience to new heights.

One such feature is the amazing 16-way power front seats with 9 massage modes that provide optimal comfort for both the driver and front passenger. All seats are made with perforated Milano premium leather that creates a first-class atmosphere. Everyone inside the vehicle is also treated to a luxurious listening experience thanks to the built-in ELS Studio 3D Signature Edition Premium Audio System with 25 speakers.

In addition to its focus on passenger comfort, the 2023 Acura MDX Type S Advance trim also offers roof rails, LED exterior door handle lights, and hidden cargo storage with a reversible smart lid. The SUV also comes with a 10.5-inch head-up display that projects vital driving information onto the windshield, allowing drivers to keep their eyes on the road while staying informed of their speed, navigation directions, and safety alerts.

Additionally, the trim includes a Power Lift Tailgate with Hands-Free Access and Walk Away Close, making it easy to access the cargo area. The Type S Advance also boasts a surround-view camera system, providing a 360-degree view of the vehicle's surroundings for easier maneuvering and parking.

With its exceptional blend of luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology, the 2023 Acura MDX Type S Advance trim stands out as the ultimate choice for drivers who demand the best in their SUV experience.

Final Thoughts

Each trim delivers a distinctive blend of style, comfort, and cutting-edge technology, ensuring an exceptional driving experience that meets and exceeds expectations. So, no matter which Acura trim level you choose, the 2023 Acura MDX promises to provide a perfect fusion of innovation, luxury, and performance that will elevate your SUV experience to new heights.
