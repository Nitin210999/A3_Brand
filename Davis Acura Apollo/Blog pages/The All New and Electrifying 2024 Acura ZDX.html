The All New and Electrifying 2024 Acura ZDX

Get ready to experience the future of luxury and performance with the upcoming 2024 Acura ZDX. This all-new and electrifying vehicle is set to redefine the automotive landscape with its advanced technology, luxurious design, and thrilling electric powertrain. From its seamless connectivity and immersive audio experience to its cutting-edge safety features and hands-free driving capabilities, the 2024 Acura ZDX is designed to deliver an unforgettable driving experience.

The Acura ZDX promises to be more than just an electric SUV—it's a symphony of style, technology, and high-performance capabilities. With a sleek silhouette, a range of up to 325 miles, and a suite of charging options, the ZDX emerges as a frontrunner in the race towards sustainable mobility. The integration of Acura's latest design direction, coupled with high-end features such as a Bang & Olufsen sound system and innovative driver-assist technologies, sets the stage for an electrifying driving experience that transcends the ordinary.

Read on as we take a closer look at what makes the 2024 Acura ZDX a symbol of Acura's unwavering commitment to ushering in a new era of electrifying and exhilarating driving.

Power and Precision Performance

The upcoming 2024 Acura ZDX is a significant milestone for Acura as it represents a crucial step towards achieving a fully electrified lineup. The all-electric 2024 Acura ZDX A-Spec will be available in both single-motor (rear wheel drive) and dual-motor (all-wheel drive) configurations. The single-motor version of the ZDX is estimated to generate around 340 horsepower, ensuring a powerful and dynamic driving experience. Additionally, it will come equipped with features such as Amplitude Reactive Damper, 20-inch aluminum alloy wheels, and all-season tires, enhancing its performance and handling capabilities.

For enthusiasts seeking the pinnacle of power and performance, they can opt for the 2024 Acura ZDX Type S. This model is touted as the most powerful and best-performing Acura SUV ever produced. Its dual-motor AWD powertrain generates an exhilarating 500 horsepower, propelling it into the realm of high-performance vehicles. The advanced features, such as Multi-link Air Suspension, High-Performance Adaptive Damper, Brembo front brakes, and 22-inch aluminum alloy wheels, contribute to its exceptional handling and responsiveness. Acura also offers a choice between all-season and summer tires, allowing drivers to optimize their ZDX Type S performance based on their driving preferences.

Additionally, Acura's claim of achieving 0-60 in under 5 seconds, as per internal testing, further solidifies the 2024 ZDX Type S as a force to be reckoned with on the road.

Moreover, the 2024 Acura ZDX lineup is built on a state-of-the-art electric vehicle platform with a flexible battery architecture. This design choice ensures a low center of gravity and near 50/50 weight distribution, enhancing the vehicle's overall stability and handling.

The Acura ZDX 2024 is expected to receive an EPA range rating of 325 zero-emission miles in the A-Spec single-motor configuration, demonstrating a commitment to practicality and sustainability. This impressive range will provide drivers with peace of mind and the ability to undertake long journeys without worrying about recharging.

Awesome Charging Options

The 2024 Acura ZDX ushers in a new era in electric vehicle charging, with its advanced charging options and capabilities. Acura has made it easy for ZDX owners to select, order, and install a charger in their homes. Clients can purchase the Acura Home Charging Station or the Portable Charging Kit, both of which are designed to provide a seamless charging experience. The battery capacity of the ZDX, at 102 kWh, contributes to its impressive, anticipated range rating of up to 325 miles.

On-the-go recharging is swift and hassle-free thanks to DC Fast Charging, offering rates of up to 190 kW. In just about 10 minutes (internal testing), the ZDX A-Spec rear-wheel drive can acquire an estimated 81 miles of range.

The Acura Home Electrification (Acura HE) is an online marketplace that helps 2024 Acura ZDX owners to connect with local installers through the support of dedicated Expert Energy Advisors. The purpose of the Acura HE is to provide clients with easy access to a network of pre-vetted local installers who can help them select the best home charging solution that fits their needs. This marketplace ensures that clients can enjoy the convenience and peace of mind afforded by having an optimized home charging solution.

2024 Acura ZDX drivers can also take advantage of the Acura Smartphone App to help them find and connect to charging stations nationwide. The app provides access to more than 90 percent of the existing DC Fast Charge networks, enabling clients to charge their vehicles quickly and efficiently while on the go. To use public charging stations, ZDX owners must have a portable charging kit.

In addition to these features, Acura EV clients will also have access to a leading high-speed brand-neutral charging network in the United States and Canada. The network is set to be one of the largest in these countries, with a target of at least 30,000 high-powered charge points by 2030. This network will ensure that Acura ZDX owners have access to fast and reliable charging options, giving them the freedom to drive longer distances and enjoy the full capabilities of their electric vehicle.

Upgraded Technology Features

The 2024 Acura ZDX features advanced, innovative technology that provide a seamless driving experience. Aside from high-end materials all throughout the cabin, the interior includes an 11-inch Precision Cockpit Driver Information Cluster that displays essential vehicle information, and an 11.3-inch infotainment screen with an intuitive touchscreen interface. The infotainment system includes wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility.

The 2024 Acura ZDX is Acura's first model to feature Google built-in, which allows for seamless on-the-go connectivity. The integration adds several features, such as Google Assistant and Google Maps, making it easy for drivers to accomplish tasks without taking their eyes off the road. These features come standard across the ZDX lineup, ensuring that drivers have access to the latest technological advancements.

The 2024 Acura ZDX elevates its luxury quotient by becoming the first Acura model to feature the immersive Bang & Olufsen sound system with an impressive 18 speakers. This partnership brings a premium audio experience to the ZDX, catering to audiophiles who appreciate top-notch sound quality while on the road.

The Acura ZDX 2024 also comes equipped with AcuraWatch, a suite of safety and driver assistive technologies. The suite includes Acura's first applications of Rear Cross Traffic Braking and Blind Zone Steering Assist, further enhancing the ZDX's safety profile.

Available for the 2024 Acura ZDX Type S is the AcuraWatch 360+, that introduces Hands Free Cruise technology. This allows drivers to experience hands-free driving on up to 400,000 miles of compatible roads. The system also comes with Automatic Parking Assist, another first in an Acura.

The Acura Precision EV Concept

The upcoming 2024 Acura ZDX boasts a luxurious and modern style that is sure to turn heads on the road. The exterior and interior design of the 2024 Acura ZDX were made by a team at the Acura Design Studio in Los Angeles, showcasing Acura's design direction introduced on the Acura Precision EV Concept.

Inside, the Acura ZDX 2024 interior has a luxury and modern design that continues to impress. The cabin is thoughtfully crafted with premium materials and a driver-oriented layout. The integration of advanced technology features, coupled with the use of high-quality materials, creates an ambiance of sophistication and comfort for both the driver and passengers.

The Acura ZDX has a low and wide stance that exudes a sporty and performance-focused look. Its sleek silhouette, long wheelbase, and high contrast surfacing demonstrate Acura's intense focus on performance from every angle. These design elements combine to create a visually striking vehicle that stands out on the road.

Unmistakable design details set the 2024 Acura ZDX apart. The front fascia features the innovation of Acura's signature design, with the iconic Chicane daytime running lights (DRLs) and Jewel Eye LED headlights. The diamond pentagon grille, with its 3-D embossed diamond pattern and illuminated surround, adds a touch of sophistication and modernity to the overall design.

In the 2024 Acura ZDX Type S variant, Acura introduces an all-new color called Double Apex Blue Pearl. This eye-catching hue adds a sense of exclusivity and sportiness to the vehicle, further enhancing its luxurious appeal. For those who prefer the signature color, the Type S will still be available in Tiger Eye Pearl, offering a timeless and elegant option.
