2024 Acura RDX Trim Levels Explained

Introducing the 2024 Acura RDX, a premium sport crossover that sets a new design, performance, and technology standard. This remarkable SUV is equipped with a powerful Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™ (SH-AWD®) system that takes its performance to new heights. Under the hood, you'll find a 2.0L VTEC Turbo Engine generating 272 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with a smooth 10-speed automatic transmission that enhances both performance and fuel efficiency.

The 2024 Acura RDX has more to offer! With five distinct trim levels, it caters to unique preferences and needs. The available trims are the standard, Technology, A-Spec®, Advance, and A-Spec® Advance. Whether you prefer style, technology, sportiness, or luxury, there is a trim level that will suit your needs. This article will guide you through each option, offering valuable insights to help you make an informed decision. Get ready to explore the stunning options and features of the 2024 Acura RDX and find the perfect trim level for you.

Standard

The RDX standard trim offers impressive features and customization options for its exterior and interior. Key highlights of this trim include advanced Jewel Eye® LED headlights, a blind spot information system, seamless wireless integration with Apple CarPlay®, and a panoramic moonroof.

On the exterior, the base trim showcases LED daytime running lights and captivating LED-illuminated dragon tail taillights. It also includes heated side mirrors and a convenient power lift tailgate. You can choose from attractive paint colors, including Fathom Blue Pearl, Lunar Silver Metallic, Majestic Black Pearl, Liquid Carbon Metallic, Performance Red Pearl, and Platinum White Pearl.

You can expect a comfortable and user-friendly experience inside the RDX base trim. It boasts dual-zone automatic climate control with humidity control, keyless access, and a modern electronic gear selector. The trim has active sound control, an automatic-dimming frameless rearview mirror, and a practical 12-volt power outlet in the front. The steering wheel is wrapped in luxurious leather, while the interior features stylish brushed aluminum trim and serene white ambient cabin lighting.

When it comes to infotainment system, the RDX Base Trim offers seamless wireless integration with Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. It also features the innovative True Touchpad Interface™ and boasts a large 10.2-inch HD dual-content center display and a convenient 7-inch color multi-information display. With the impressive Acura Premium Audio System and its nine speakers, you can enjoy a high-quality sound experience.

Safety and driver assistance are also prioritized in the RDX base trim. It includes essential features such as the Collision Mitigation Braking System™ (CMBS™), advanced pedestrian detection, adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist system (LKAS), road departure mitigation (RDM) system, traffic sign recognition, auto high-beam headlights, hill start assist, and automatic brake hold. Moreover, this trim is built with the next-generation ACE™ body structure for enhanced safety. It also has a multi-view rear camera with dynamic guidelines, a blind spot information system, and a rear cross-traffic monitor to assist you on the road.

Technology

The RDX Technology trim elevates luxury to the next level with its impressive features and meticulous attention to detail. Notable highlights of this trim include the Acura navigation system, ELS STUDIO® Premium Audio, premium leather-trimmed interior, and a convenient wireless charging pad.

The Technology trim boasts stunning 19-inch pewter gray metallic machine-finished split 5-spoke wheels that add sophistication to its exterior. As an added option, the captivating Phantom Violet Pearl paint joins the existing color choices, providing a unique and eye-catching selection for those seeking to make a statement.

Inside the RDX, comfort and convenience reach new heights. The inclusion of GPS-Linked Climate Control ensures precise temperature regulation, allowing passengers to enjoy personalized comfort during their journey.

When it comes to technology and connectivity, the Technology Trim offers advanced features to keep drivers informed and entertained. The Acura navigation system with a 3D view provides seamless navigation, offering real-time traffic updates and intuitive guidance. The wireless charging pad also conveniently keeps mobile devices powered up, eliminating the hassle of dealing with tangled cords.

A-Spec ®

The RDX A-Spec® trim combines style and performance in an irresistible package designed to make a lasting impression. This model stands out with its sport appearance package, ELS STUDIO 3D® premium audio, Ultrasuede®-trimmed interior, and LED fog lights. The 20-inch shark gray split 5-spoke wheels add to the sporty look of the Acura RDX A Spec trim. Additionally, the striking Apex Blue Pearl color is introduced as a new option, enhancing its visual appeal.

Inside, the A-Spec® trim offers a range of interior enhancements that elevate the driving experience to a premium level. The Y-pattern dark brushed aluminum trim, ebony headliner, and sport pedals contribute to the performance-oriented aesthetic.

To ensure maximum comfort, the A-Spec® trim includes ventilated front seats that cool drivers and passengers on hot days. The sport seats are upgraded with perforated Milano premium leather, and Ultrasuede® trimmed interiors, featuring contrast stitching and piping for a touch of luxury and sportiness.

Advance

With a focus on advanced technology and luxurious comfort, the RDX Advance trim offers an array of premium features. One prominent feature is the surround-view camera, providing a comprehensive view of the vehicle's surroundings. The head-up display projects essential information onto the windshield, ensuring drivers can keep their eyes on the road. Ventilated and heated front seats guarantee optimal comfort regardless of the weather conditions. The adaptive damper system adjusts the suspension to deliver a smooth and controlled ride.

Expanding upon the shared features of the RDX lineup, the Advance trim incorporates additional enhancements to elevate the overall driving experience. LED puddle lights and power-folding side mirrors enhance visibility and convenience, making it easier to navigate in low-light conditions. Inside the cabin, the heated steering wheel offers added warmth during chilly days, while the extra 12-volt power outlet in the rear cargo area allows for convenient charging of devices. The brushed aluminum trim is replaced by authentic open-pore wood trim, adding a touch of sophistication and refinement to the interior aesthetic.

Taking luxury and comfort up a notch from the A-Spec® trim, the RDX Advance trim introduces heated rear seats for the outboard positions, ensuring all passengers are cozy throughout the journey. Upgraded front seats with 16-way power adjustments, including power lumbar support, thigh extension, and side bolsters, offer personalized comfort and support for the driver and front passenger. These features create a truly advanced and luxurious driving experience in the RDX Advance trim.

A-Spec® Advance

Combining the best features of the A-Spec® and Advance trim levels, the RDX A-Spec® Advance offers a range of exciting highlights, including the adaptive damper system, 16-way power front sport seats, heated rear seats, and LED puddle lights. The A-Spec® Advance is equipped with 20-inch shark gray split multi-spoke wheels that enhance the vehicle's exterior appearance.

On the interior, the RDX A-Spec® Advance adds an extra 12-volt power outlet in the rear cargo area and replaces the brushed aluminum trim with a Y-pattern dark brushed aluminum trim. The A-Spec® Advance also offers three interior color choices: ebony, red, orchid, providing a personalized touch to the cabin.

Building upon the A-Spec® trim, the A-Spec® Advance takes luxury and comfort to the next level. The sport seats feature perforated Milano premium leather and Ultrasuede® trimmed interiors.

In addition to these features, the A-Spec® Advance offers a 10.5-inch heads-up display (HUD), providing convenience and enhancing driving safety. It also includes a surround-view camera system, head-up warning (HUW), rain-sensing windshield wipers, and windshield wiper de-icer, further improving the driving experience.

Final Note

On a final note, the 2024 Acura RDX offers five trim levels, each with unique features and enhancements. Whether you prioritize luxury, performance, or advanced technology, the Acura RDX trim levels have something to offer. From the standard to the A-Spec® Advance trim, there are options to suit every driver's preferences. So, explore the Acura RDX trims today to find the perfect fit for you.
