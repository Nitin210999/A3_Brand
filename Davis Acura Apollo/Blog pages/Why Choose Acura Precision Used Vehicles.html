Why Choose Acura Precision Used Vehicles?

Acura is a luxury vehicle division of the renowned Japanese automaker Honda. Their Acura Precision Used Vehicles are pre-owned cars that undergo rigorous, multi-point inspection and certification processes to ensure they meet the high standards of quality and reliability that Acura is known for. With their sleek design, advanced technology, and premium features, Acura Precision Used Vehicles offer exceptional value and performance to their owners.

Another reason why Acura Precision Used Vehicles are a great choice is their outstanding reputation for durability and longevity. Acura is known for producing vehicles that are built to last, with many models exceeding 100,000 miles without major issues. Backed by precision engineering, their vehicles deliver exceptional performance, safety, and comfort.

Additionally, buying an Acura Precision Used Vehicle allows you to get behind the wheel of a high-quality, luxury vehicle at a much lower price point than buying a new car. It also comes with a comprehensive warranty and roadside assistance program, providing peace of mind to buyers!

Trusted Luxury Brand

Over the years, Acura has established a reputation as a trusted luxury car maker. Firstly, as a subsidiary of Honda — a brand known for its reliability and quality — they inherit its standards and values, making it a reliable brand for luxury car buyers. Their vehicles are known for their longevity, making them a smart investment for those who want a car that can last for years without major issues.

Another reason why is Acura’s commitment to innovation and technology. Their cars feature advanced safety and driver assistance technologies that ensure a safe and comfortable driving experience. Their engineers work tirelessly to develop new technologies that improve the driving experience, such as their Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system, which improves handling and stability in all driving conditions.

They also have a strong reputation for customer satisfaction. The brand's commitment to providing excellent customer service and support ensures that customers feel valued and cared for throughout their ownership experience. Acura's dealerships are known for their professionalism, expertise, and friendly approach, making the car-buying process a pleasant experience.

Carefully Inspected Vehicles

Acura Precision Used Vehicles are meticulously inspected to ensure that they meet the high standards of quality and reliability that customers expect from the Acura brand. The inspection process starts with a comprehensive 112-point inspection (180-point for Acura Precision Certified Pre-Owned cars) that covers many aspects of the vehicle, from the engine and transmission to the interior and exterior. This inspection is conducted by Acura-trained technicians who have extensive knowledge of the brand's engineering and design standards.

During the inspection, the technicians use specialized diagnostic equipment to check for any issues with the vehicle's mechanical systems. They also inspect the body and exterior for any signs of wear and tear, as well as the interior for any damage or issues with the electronics and other features. Once the inspection is complete, the technicians make any necessary repairs or replacements using only genuine Acura parts.

The rigorous inspection process and warranty coverage ensure that every car in Acura's Certified Pre-Owned catalog (aka Acura CPO inventory) is among the highest quality and most reliable used cars on the market. So even if you check the vehicle's history report, you can rest assured that it was properly taken care of.

Warranty Coverage

The comprehensive warranty program offered by Acura for their Precision Used vehicles provides coverage for certain components of the vehicle. Some of the features include:

Limited Warranty Coverage: The Acura Precision Used Vehicle warranty provides coverage for up to 6 months or 7,500 total odometer miles, whichever comes first. This means that if any covered component of the vehicle fails during this time, Acura will repair it at no cost to the owner.

Comprehensive Component Coverage: The warranty covers a wide range of components, including the engine, transmission, axle, brakes, steering, suspension, electrical system, heating and cooling systems, and more.

Roadside Assistance: Acura's Precision Used Vehicle warranty includes 24-hour roadside assistance for the duration of the warranty. This service provides assistance for emergency situations such as a flat tire, dead battery, or lockout.

Trip-Interruption Reimbursement: It is designed to provide assistance to Acura owners who experience a mechanical breakdown while on a trip 100 miles away from home. This can provide up to $100 per day for up to three days for expenses related to lodging, meals, and transportation.

Concierge Service: This includes urgent message relay, emergency airline reservations & ticketing assistance, 24-hour weather information, arrangement for the emergency shipment of personal items, and more.

Transferable: The Acura Precision Used Vehicle warranty is fully transferable to subsequent owners, which can increase the resale value of the vehicle.

In summary, the warranty provides peace of mind for buyers of certified pre-owned Acura vehicles, knowing that they are protected against unexpected repair costs.
