The most advanced Subaru we’ve ever built.

The 2024 Subaru Solterra gives you a new way to explore our world. It features an advanced all-electric, zero-emission StarDrive® electric powertrain and uses the e-Subaru Global Platform. Plus, Solterra is the first AWD electric vehicle to feature our legendary standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive. It’s also our most advanced SUV ever, featuring a charge time reduced by about half and improved cold-weather charging for 2024*. There’s state-of-the-art technology like a stunning available 12.3-inch touchscreen and a standard DriverFocus® Distraction Mitigation System*, along with the segment-leading capability of 8.3 inches of ground clearance.

Love Your Subaru and the Earth

The Solterra is our first zero-emission all-electric SUV.

The Solterra lets you explore the outdoors and help protect it at the same time. The advanced StarDrive electric powertrain means no tailpipe emissions and no need for gasoline or oil changes, so every mile has significantly less impact on the environment. The Solterra is the latest example of our commitment to safeguarding the natural world we all share through sustainable transportation — and it’s just the beginning.

Eco-Friendly with Upgraded Charging

Improved DC Fast Charging

The 2024 Solterra can fast-charge in about half the time as the previous model, now taking about 35 minutes to charge from 10% to 80% using its built-in fast-charge capability*. The StarDrive powertrain gives Solterra an EPA-estimated range of up to 227 miles* with standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive as well as a variety of options for fast and convenient charging, both at home and on the go. The Solterra comes with a charger that works with any standard 120V outlet for Level 1 charging. You can also have a 240V Level 2 charger installed at home so you can plug in overnight and wake up each morning with a full charge*.

Improved Cold-Weather Charging

Charging your Solterra is fast and easy even when you’re heading out for cold-weather fun. The upgraded charging system on the 2024 Solterra now charges two times faster at 32 degrees and four times faster at 14 degrees*, helping keep you on the go no matter the conditions.

Free Subaru Just Drive Car Rentals

For extended cross-country road trips or backcountry expeditions way off the grid, Solterra owners get 10 free days of Subaru Just Drive car rentals. With Just Drive, you can choose from a selection of vehicles in the Subaru fleet and rent directly from a participating Subaru retailer. With Just Drive, you’re not just getting a rental car; you’re getting the extra peace of mind that comes with all the safety, capability, and quality Subaru vehicles are known for.

Conserving Our Parks

As the largest corporate donor to the National Park Foundation, Subaru has a long history of commitment to protecting our nation’s greatest treasures, helping conserve over 85 million acres of land in America’s more than 400 national parks. Subaru has provided over $70 million to organizations working to conserve our parks, helping fund efforts such as recycling, composting, and waste reduction operations. Subaru has also donated its zero-landfill expertise and funding to the parks to help them become more sustainable through the Subaru “Don’t Feed the Landfills” initiative.

Quality Engineering

It may be the first of its kind, but the Solterra is still a Subaru at heart. You can trust a Subaru vehicle for miles and miles of adventure — 96% of Subaru vehicles sold in the last 10 years are still on the road today, more than Honda or Toyota brands*.

Standard EyeSight® Driver Assist Technologies

Subaru EyeSight

Every 2024 Solterra features standard EyeSight Driver Assist Technologies, which act like an extra set of eyes on the road and, if need be, an extra foot on the brake when you drive7. These advanced active safety systems monitor traffic movement, optimize cruise control, and warn you when you’re swaying outside your lane.*

Pre-Collision system

The Pre-Collision System* can apply full braking force and bring you to a complete stop in emergency situations.

Traffic Jam Assist

Traffic Jam Assist* helps make traveling through congestion safer and easier by providing hands-free steering assistance with driver supervision in low-speed traffic jams (up to 25 MPH) on most major highways. Dynamic Radar Cruise Control* and Lane Tracing Assist* can take some of the stress out of driving by helping with steering, braking, and throttle control — both in daily traffic and on long road trips.

DriverFocus Distraction Mitigation System and Front Cross-Traffic Alert

Working in conjunction with EyeSight, the standard DriverFocus Distraction Mitigation System*, new for 2024, is like having an attentive co-pilot along for the ride, available to give a helpful alert if a driver becomes distracted or drowsy. This system is designed to enhance the effectiveness of other safety systems while also increasing passenger safety within the vehicle. If its steering-column-mounted camera detects a distracted, drowsy, or incapacitated driver, it will provide an alert. DriverFocus also combines with other safety systems such as Emergency Driving Stop System by allowing them to intervene earlier when necessary. Front Cross-Traffic Alert*, also new for 2024, watches for unexpected traffic from either side and can alert you to approaching vehicles — including those that may not be visible from the driver’s seat.

DriverFocus standard on: All trims
Front Cross-Traffic Alert standard on: All trims

Panoramic View Monitor

Panoramic View Monitor is an available 360-degree surround-view camera system that gives a clear picture of the objects around your Solterra, delivering enhanced awareness and safety when parking and reversing.

Standard on: Limited and Touring

All-Around Awareness Technologies

The 2024 Solterra features a comprehensive suite of active safety features to help protect you on every drive. The Blind-Spot Monitor* system warns you with a visual indicator on each side mirror if it senses a vehicle in your blind spots. New for 2024, Front Cross-Traffic Alert can alert you if unexpected vehicles — even vehicles you may not be able to see — approach from either side of your Solterra. Lane Change Assist*, another new feature, watches for other vehicles as you move into a new lane, helps with steering, and automatically cancels your turn signal. Rear Cross-Traffic Alert* helps warn you of traffic approaching from the side as you back up. And with Parking Support Brake, the Solterra can even stop itself to help avoid objects behind you*.

Standard on: All trims

Safe Exit Assist

Safe Exit Assist, a standard safety technology on Solterra, will beep and display a message if it detects approaching vehicles or moving objects before you open your door*. It’s just one more way the Solterra watches out for you — even after you’ve arrived at your destination.

Standard on: All trims

Adventurous and Versatile SUV

Standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive

As the first-ever all-electric SUV to feature the capability of Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive, the Solterra takes this legendary all-weather, all-terrain traction system to exciting new places. The balanced design of Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive creates uniform stability and delivers an optimal distribution of power for maximum traction and confident handling. Couple that with the smooth linear output of the dual electric motors in the Solterra 5-seater SUV and you have outstanding traction on all kinds of surfaces and weather conditions.

8.3 Inches of Ground Clearance and X-MODE®

The 2024 Solterra has class-leading ground clearance and short overhangs for easy maneuverability over rough terrain so you can go places traditional electric vehicles typically can’t reach. Standard dual-function X-MODE with settings for snow/dirt and deep snow/mud lets you choose the perfect traction setting to match conditions for enhanced stability and control, even while going down hills. It’s everything you need in an electric off-road SUV.

Subaru StarDrive® with Driving Mode Select and S-Pedal

Subaru StarDrive offers an enhanced driving experience through Driving Mode Select, which lets you choose from Normal Mode for typical trips, Eco Mode to conserve battery power, and an extra-responsive Power Mode. This advanced technology is engineered with the latest breakthroughs in electric vehicle technology to deliver substantial on-demand torque at all speeds for exciting everyday driving. Steering-wheel-mounted paddles give the ability to dial in your preferred level of regenerative braking, while S-Pedal Drive adds even more braking to help reduce the fatigue of switching between gas and brake pedals*.

Advanced Park is an available automatic self-parking system that can be engaged while at the wheel to take the hassle out of parking.

By just saying, “Hey, Subaru,” you can keep your hands on the wheel and let the assistant handle the rest.
