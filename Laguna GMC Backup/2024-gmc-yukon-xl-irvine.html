Introducing the 2024 Yukon & Yukon XL Irvine, CA from Laguna Niguel.

The 2024 Yukon XL is truly a symbol of excellence and is perhaps among the leading models in the running for the ultimate full-sized SUV. From its classic sterling metallic exterior to the Alpine Umber interior, this vehicle definitely has everything there is to offer from a top-tier SUV. The exterior boasts a front-facing camera on the bonnet, LED headlights, fog lights, daytime running lights, and also tail lights. It’s a rear-wheel drive that can go into four-wheel drive with tires 275 on its width and a 50 series side wall wrapped around 22-inch wheels. This vehicle boasts a lot of head and leg room, and a lot of cargo capacity. The rear view is very clean, having retractable window wipers and a quad-tip exhaust. Underneath the hood, you get a 420 horsepower engine, 460 pound-feet of torque, and can do a 10-speed automatic auto transmission.

Moving on to the interior, this vehicle is a true behemoth with a cargo capacity of up to 144.7 cubic feet. This cargo space can be maximized using two sets of buttons in the rear wall which will lower the third-row and middle-row seats, creating more space and essentially turning this ultimate full-size SUV into the ultimate tailgating full-size SUV. The interior doors also boast a nice armrest that’s unusually large thanks to the size of the vehicle and a beautiful premium wood grain paneling.

At the front seats, the vehicle comes with, premium leather seats which are power-adjustable, heated, and ventilated, with speakers built into the headrest for that premium experience. The driver’s seat is not left out of this experience, boasting a power-adjustable steering wheel for tilt and telescope adjustments, a head-up display, and a premium multitasker with multiple functionalities. This monster of a vehicle is truly an ultimate, and comes as no surprise that it can tow up to 8200 pounds of load.
