What Makes the 2024 GMC Yukon XL the Best Large SUV

The automotive landscape is evolving, and in the realm of large SUVs, the 2024 GMC Yukon XL emerges as a standout, redefining what it means to be the best in its class.

As we delve into the intricacies of this impressive vehicle, it becomes evident that the Yukon XL is more than just a spacious family hauler; it's a powerhouse of innovation and performance. From its commanding presence on the road to the cutting-edge technologies seamlessly integrated into its design, the 2024 Yukon XL is poised to set a new standard for what discerning drivers expect from a large SUV.

At the heart of the GMC Yukon XL's appeal lies its exceptional powertrain options, each carefully engineered to cater to a spectrum of driving preferences. With an engine that delivers an impressive horsepower, the Yukon XL establishes itself as a force to be reckoned with.

Beyond sheer power, the Yukon XL sets itself apart through a meticulous attention to detail in its design and features. A long wheelbase enhances both the exterior aesthetics and interior spaciousness, ensuring a comfortable journey for all occupants. Technological marvels such as advanced driver assistance systems and an intuitive infotainment interface further solidify the Yukon XL's status as a technologically advanced marvel on wheels.

As we explore what makes the 2024 GMC Yukon XL the best large SUV, it becomes clear that this vehicle isn't just a means of transportation; it's a statement of excellence in the world of full-sized, family-friendly SUVs.

Capable Machine

The 2024 GMC Yukon XL is a force to be reckoned with in the realm of power and performance, offering an unparalleled driving experience both in urban settings and off-road adventures.

At its core, the Yukon XL is equipped with a potent 5.3-liter V8 engine that roars to life with an impressive 355 horsepower and 383 lb.-ft. of torque. This powerhouse is seamlessly paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission, ensuring a smooth and dynamic ride. The incorporation of Direct Injection and Variable Valve Timing further refines the engine's efficiency, allowing the Yukon XL to deliver an outstanding performance on various terrains.

For those seeking even greater efficiency and an extended range, the Yukon XL offers an optional 3.0-liter DURAMAX Turbo Diesel engine. With 277 horsepower and an impressive 460 lb.-ft. of torque, this diesel engine not only provides substantial towing capacity but also offers a maximum highway range of up to 756 miles on a full tank, underscoring its efficiency and long-distance capabilities.

The Yukon also has a maximum towing capacity of 8400 pounds (on a rear-wheel-drive model) that makes hauling a trailer or a boat easy.

For enthusiasts looking for top-tier performance, the Yukon XL offers a 6.2-liter V8 engine as an option on select trims such as the AT4, Denali, and Denali Ultimate. This powerhouse generates an impressive 420 horsepower and 460 lb.-ft. of torque, making it the most powerful engine available for the vehicle. Whether it's for towing, long-distance cruising, or a city commute, the 2024 GMC Yukon XL provides a range of powertrain options to suit diverse preferences and needs.

Thoughtful Design

The 2024 GMC Yukon XL showcases an awesome design that seamlessly blends aesthetic appeal with practicality. Stepping into the interior, the Yukon XL impresses with a sleek ambiance complemented by aluminum details and accent stitching throughout. The flexible seating and storage configurations provide adaptability to diverse needs, accommodating up to 8 passengers comfortably. Even with all seats in use, the Yukon XL doesn't compromise on cargo space, offering a generous 41.5 cubic feet behind the third row with seats folded.

For those seeking additional luxury and convenience, the SLT trim introduces features like a panoramic sunroof, a Power-Sliding Center Console that slides back for extra storage, second-row power-release fold-and-tumble bucket seats, and third-row power fold-flat seats that create an impressive rear opening and load floor for cargo.

Taking luxury a step further, the Denali Ultimate trim elevates the interior with full-grain leather 16-way power front seats featuring massage features, heated and ventilated front seats, a heated steering wheel, heated second-row seats, and an Alpine Umber interior adorned with laser-etched accents and an exclusive front seat metal badging.

Externally, the 2024 GMC Yukon XL boasts a distinctive and commanding presence. The unique grille with a chrome frame and LED headlamps featuring GMC's signature C-shaped design accentuates the Yukon's bold aesthetic. Premium 18 to 22-inch wheels, depending on the model, enhance both style and performance. With an overall vehicle length of 225.2 inches, the Yukon XL stands out with its larger dimensions, providing not only style but also enhanced practicality for a diverse range of activities.

The Denali and Denali Ultimate models further distinguish themselves with a dual exhaust system sporting dual twin polished stainless-steel tips. The 2024 GMC Yukon XL is a testament to the brand's commitment to blending sophistication and functionality in a large SUV.

Tech Capabilities

The 2024 GMC Yukon XL is a technological marvel, integrating advanced features that elevate the driving experience to the next level.

At the heart of its tech-savvy interior is a 10.2-inch diagonal premium GMC infotainment system, which not only provides access to a plethora of apps and features but also seamlessly integrates with popular smartphone platforms through Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. The inclusion of voice-activated technology ensures that drivers can effortlessly control various functions, promoting a safer and more convenient driving experience.

Enhancing the driver's interface, the Yukon XL features a 12-inch diagonal digital Driver Information Center, allowing for easy access to vital information and control functions directly from the steering wheel. The SLT trim takes the technological experience even further, offering 12.6-inch diagonal rear-seat media touchscreens, wireless charging capabilities, HD vehicle surround vision, and a premium Bose Audio System.

Moving up to the AT4 trim, Denali, and Denali Ultimate variants introduces a cutting-edge 15-inch diagonal Head-Up Display, providing key information directly within the driver's line of sight for enhanced safety and convenience.

The Yukon XL doesn't just stop at entertainment and convenience; it excels in advanced safety and driver assistance features. Depending on the model, the Yukon XL can be equipped with up to 13 camera views, including features like Transparent Trailer View, Hitch Guidance with Hitch View, Surround View, and more.

The Denali and Denali Ultimate variants take safety to the next level with the introduction of the Super Cruise system, marking a significant stride in hands free driving technology. Under the right conditions and with an attentive driver, Super Cruise allows for true hands-free operation on compatible roads, exemplifying GMC's commitment to cutting-edge technology.

Driver and Passenger Safety

The 2024 GMC Yukon XL prioritizes safety with a comprehensive suite of advanced technologies designed to enhance driver awareness and facilitate collision avoidance.

The foundation of this safety commitment lies in the GMC Pro Safety System, an integrated package of features that work seamlessly to mitigate potential risks. This system includes Automatic Emergency Braking, Following Distance Indicator, Forward Collision Alert, Front Pedestrian Braking, IntelliBeam, and Lane Keep Assist with Lane Departure Warning.

For enhanced safety coverage, the GMC Pro Safety Plus System builds upon the Pro Safety features by incorporating additional elements. This advanced safety package includes:

Lane Change Alert with Side Blind Zone Alert – helps drivers make safer lane changes by providing alerts if a vehicle is detected in their blind spot.

Rear Cross Traffic Alert – designed to mitigate the risk of collisions while backing out of parking spaces by providing alerts if cross traffic is detected behind the vehicle.

Front and Rear Park Assist – provides a stress-free parking experience by offering warnings as the vehicle approaches nearby objects.

Always remember that driver assistance features like the ones above cannot replace the driver's responsibility to operate the vehicle safely. It is crucial for you as the driver to stay attentive to traffic, surroundings, and road conditions at all times.

Conclusion

The 2024 GMC Yukon XL stands out as one of the best large SUVs on the market today. With its impressive power, tech features and advanced safety systems, it offers a truly elevated driving experience. Whether it's for long road trips or daily commutes, the Yukon XL proves itself as a standout option in the large SUV segment, making it a top choice for those seeking the best in class.
