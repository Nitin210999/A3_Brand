HEAD-UP DISPLAY

Help keep your focus on the road with the Head-Up Display, which conveniently projects customizable vehicle info within the driver’s view:

  • Speed Limit
  • Driving Speed
  • Drive Mode
  • Audio
  • Engine RPM

You can even adjust the height, info displayed, brightness and image rotation.

HIGH DEFINITION SURROUND VISION

This feature uses multiple cameras to display a digital overhead image of the area around your vehicle along with the Rear Vision Camera* or front views.

High Definition Surround Vision* is also designed to help you park and avoid nearby objects. You can select additional views with guidance lines on your camera display, including:

  • Front and rear standard views
  • Front and rear cross-traffic views
  • Front and rear overhead views
  • Front and rear bowl views
  • Side forward and rearward views
  • Hitch View (if equipped)
  • Top down view
